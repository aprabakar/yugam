About us

Yugam has snowballed into one of the largest Techno cultural fest in South India.
The fest, an initiative by the students of Kumaraguru College of Technology
has grown exponentially, raising the bars to new heights every year.
So,the stage is set and the crowd is calling !
Yugam 2017 is here!
A massive platform for providing Techno cultural exchange among students.
Gear up with us for this exalted annual celebration !
Yugam is the place to be. All roads lead to Kumaraguru College of Technology this February 2017!
